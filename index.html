A short course with Daniel Thorson & Michal Tolk

Values-Aligned AI

Building systems that serve your life — not the other way around.

AI is changing everything. But most of what's being taught about AI is about productivity — doing more, faster, with less friction.

We're interested in a different question: What if AI could help you live a more beautiful life?

Not a more optimized life. Not a more efficient life. A life more aligned with what you actually care about — your values, your relationships, your health, your creative work, your spiritual practice.

We've spent the last year building AI systems for ourselves — not as a tech experiment, but as a genuine practice of aligning our tools with our deepest intentions. What we've discovered has surprised us. These systems don't just save time. They mirror back what matters. They surface what you're avoiding. They hold the threads you keep dropping.

In this short course, we'll share what we've learned and give you the space to build your own.

What We'll Cover

Session 1: The View

  • Why most AI advice misses what matters
  • What "values-aligned" actually means in practice
  • How we built systems that reflect our values — not just our to-do lists
  • A live experiment: using AI in a specific way to evoke your own clarity
  • Hands-on: beginning to design your own system

Session 2: Reflection & Build

  • Share what you built and what you noticed
  • Troubleshoot together — we learn from each other's friction
  • Two pathways: Notion + Claude (simple) or a full agent setup (advanced)
  • How to keep your system alive and evolving

This isn't a course about prompts or productivity hacks. It's about learning to use the most powerful tools ever created in service of what you actually love.

Who This Is For

You don't need to be technical. You don't need prior AI experience. You just need to care about living deliberately — and be curious about whether these tools could support that.

  • Coaches, healers, and practitioners who want AI that fits their values
  • Writers, creatives, and entrepreneurs building a life around meaning
  • Anyone who feels the pull of AI but doesn't want to lose their soul to it
  • Couples or partners who want to build shared systems together
