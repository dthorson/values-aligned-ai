A short course with Daniel Thorson & Michal Tolk

Values-Aligned AI

Building systems that serve your life — not the other way around.

Maybe you've heard a lot about AI and felt vaguely like it isn't for you. Like it's a tool for tech people, or productivity obsessives, or people with a very different kind of life than yours.

We felt that way too, for a long time.

What changed for us wasn't finding a better app or a more impressive demo. It was discovering that AI can actually listen — that if you learn to talk to it in a particular way, it reflects back your own thinking with a clarity that's genuinely hard to find elsewhere. It asks good questions. It helps you hear what you actually want.

For coaches, healers, writers, and people doing meaningful work in the world, that turns out to be remarkably useful. Not for doing more. For living better.

This course is a gentle on-ramp. Two sessions, a small group, two guides who will walk in alongside you. No technical background required. No pressure to become an AI person. Just an honest exploration of whether this tool might support the life you're already trying to live.

What We'll Do Together

Session 1: Finding Your Way In

  • How to have a real conversation with AI — not just issue commands
  • Learning to ask for what you actually want (this is harder and more powerful than it sounds)
  • A few simple ideas for orienting yourself — how to think about context, how to let the conversation clarify what you're after, why speaking out loud often works better than typing
  • A first look at building a simple life system grounded in your values and intentions
  • A live exploration together: you'll leave having felt what this can actually do

Between Sessions

A simple homework: using what you learned, begin building a space that reflects your values, your intentions, and your life — somewhere you can return to and deepen over time.

Session 2: What Did You Notice?

  • We share what we built and what we noticed
  • Troubleshoot together — the stuck places are where the learning lives
  • How to keep going from here

The goal isn't to become someone who's "good at AI." It's to find out whether this tool can support the life you're already trying to live.

Who This Is For

This course is for people who sense that AI might be relevant to their lives — but haven't found a way in that feels like them.

  • Coaches, therapists, healers, and practitioners
  • Writers and creative people
  • Anyone doing meaningful work who wants more support, not more noise
  • People who are curious but overwhelmed — and would rather learn in good company
